Mission / Manifesto

Zen0 is born from curiosity — the need to explore, break, and understand systems at their core. I see hacking as a language: a sharp and honest way to test the real resilience of digital environments. No polished interface can cover a well-placed flaw. This is my offensive side: where logic meets chaos to build something better.

Approach

I don't follow checklists. I don’t simulate risk, I explore it. Every target is different. Every test starts with a single question: "If I wanted in, where would I start?" From there, I observe, I test, I adapt. I look for human errors, blind spots, assumptions. When I find the crack, I document it—not to exploit, but to teach where no one else was looking.
